About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University (NJU), advised by Prof. Yibiao Yang and Prof. Lei Xu. In June 2021, I obtained my M.Sc. from the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) under the supervision of Prof. Yibiao Yang and Prof. Bin Yuan. Prior to that, I received my B.Sc. from the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at Wuhan University of Technology (WUT) in June 2019.
My research focuses on the analysis and testing of system software. If you are interested in my work or would like to collaborate, please feel free to contact me!
